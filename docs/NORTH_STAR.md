# North star metric

**Weekly Active Planners** — the number of users who finished at least one task in the last 7 days.

## Why this one

I thought about using signups but that just measures traffic, not whether the app actually did anything useful. Session count felt gaming-able too. A completed task means the user sat down, did something and marked it done, which is the whole point of the app.

It also ties naturally to everything else I care about. If onboarding is better, more people reach their first task. If retention improves, more people come back next week. The number goes up when the product gets better.

## What I watch alongside it

A few things I'd check so the number doesn't mislead me:

* Are tasks being completed in under a minute? That might mean users are just ticking boxes.
* Is the support inbox getting worse? If WAP goes up but people are frustrated, something's off.

## Where it shows up

The Overview tab shows the current week's number and the trend over the last 12 weeks.
